whit (hwit, wit)

n. The least bit; a small particle, accomplishment, or change.

Is the point at which you stand WHERE you truly want to be? Have you ever HOPED you could be someplace else, in a different setting, but you lacked the INSPIRATION to take the next step so you could ultimately TRIUMPH over your goals? Dowhit.com creates an opportunity for you to change your WHERE, and bring your HOPES closer by INSPIRING you to reach the ultimate TRIUMPH.

Where

    Any spot where you can turn your head and see what you have accomplished.

    Everyone, everything, and everyplace is constantly moving toward a final destination

Hope

    Some people go to sleep with hope they wake up while others wake up with the hope they can get back to sleep.

    The lighted point at the end of a long dark path that we deliberately stride toward with the hope that this next stride might bring us into the light at the end of that path.

Inspiration

    That feeling that washes over everyone at some point. The driving force behind the pursuit of all aspirations.

    Inspiration is finding a way to create a path from Hope to Triumph

    The revelation to become better by doing, creating, constructing and experiencing.

Triumph

    The completion of whits. The single moment where all pressures are released and the pride of accomplishments overwhelms the soul.

    The ascendancy of the inspirational mountain laid before you from hope to where you stand, arms held high in triumph.

Design Process

The Value of Design

Design is based on the inspiration of past accomplishments. On that foundation, we can build upon those achievements to shape the future. Design is about life - past, present and future - and the learning process that happens between birth and death. It is about community and shared knowledge and experience. It is the passion to build on what we've learned to create something better.

Design Influences

About this Site

The words "design influences" can be understood as both a plural noun and as subject and verb. The plural noun speaks of those who have come before us and paved the way. The verb speaks of the responsibility of design to lead the way. By understanding where we have come from, we have a better idea of where we are going and, perhaps, where we should be heading.

Inspiration

Inspired by the Past

I have been inspired by the work of many who have pioneered advances in Web Standards, including Jesse Bennett-Chamberlain, Douglas Bowman, Allen Chang, Andy Clarke, Jon Hicks, Shaun Inman, Cameron Moll, Veerle Pieters, Jason Santa Maria, Dave Shea, Ryan Sims, Nathan Smith, and Jeffrey Zeldman, to name a few. Thank you for inspiring me to give something back.

Contribution

Shaping the Future

The Fluid 960 Grid System templates have been built upon the work of Nathan Smith and his 960 Grid System using effects from the Mootools JavaScript library. The idea for building these templates was inspired by Andy Clarke, author of Transcending CSS, who advocates a content-out approach to rapid interactive prototyping, crediting Jason Santa Maria with the grey box method.
